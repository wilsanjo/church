About Us

Get to know about Voice Of Ministry international.

The voice of victory ministry is a Bible and scriptural base church that teaches the uncompromise message and pray directly by the scriptures. The ministry is a pantecoastal by denomination.

Our primary goal is to see the lost souls be given back to Christ.
